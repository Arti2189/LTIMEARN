Project Description:-  
It's a restaurant recommendation website.The user can search restaurant on the basis of name and city.
Each restaurant contains the details like its address, postal code, website, rating etc.

Documents:10000
fields : 9

Field Details:
1.address:-Restaurant address
2.City:-City of Restaurant
3.Country:-Country of Restaurant
4.latitude:-Latitude of Restaurant Location
5.longitude:-Longitude of Restaurant Location
6.name:-Name of Restaurant
7.rating:-Rating of Restaurant
8.postalCode:-Postal Code of Restaurant
9.websites:-Website of Restaurant
