
* This site has pages devoted to individual locations, such as restaurants or schools,Where users can submit reviews for their products or services from one to five star rating system.
* Users can view restaurant reservations in Reservations category.
* This site added features for exploring and scheduling manicures, flowers, golf and legal consultations, among other things, through    website.
* Exploring Hotels is also a added featured in this site.
* This dataset have one collection containing described below fields:

  1. buisness_id : primary key to the collection 
  2. name: name of the shop
  3. neighborhood: place near by shop
  4. address: address of shop
  5. city: City where you want to search
  6. state: state in which your city is
  7. postal_code: postal code of city
  8. latitude:latitiude of shop address
  9. longitude:longitude of shop address
  10.stars: it is a rating between 1 to 5 updated by user
  11.review_count: count of reviews for this shop
  12.is_open: it tells that the shop is open or not. 0 for closed and 1 for open
  13.categories: it will be our search criteria according to categories which include spa,bars,restaurant,hotels,outdoors,schools,
     college,hospitals,crockery,stationary,air conditioning and many more daily life events.
