Project Description:-  
It's a restaurant recommendation website. People can search restaurants on the basis of Zonal area, City and Name of the Restaurant and help them find a favourable place to eat according to their needs.
Each restaurant contains details like its address, postal code, website, rating etc.

Documents:10000
fields : 11

Field Details:
1.address:-Restaurant address
2.City:-City of Restaurant
3.Zonal Region:-Zone in which Restaurant is located
4.Province:-State of Restaurant
5.Country:-Country of Restaurant
6.latitude:-Latitude of Restaurant Location
7.longitude:-Longitude of Restaurant Location
8.name:-Name of Restaurant
9.rating:-Rating of Restaurant
10.postalCode:-Postal Code of Restaurant
11.websites:-Website of Restaurant
